I am a jack of all trades and I love to Learn!

I absolutely love learning new things such as (and not limited to) coding to athletics and even cooking. In this day and age, we have what is called the internet, a wonderful place, providing us with the power to research and share knowledge with people from all over the world! When I encounter something I do not understand or something I'm interested in, the first thing I do is research. Even this website was made from learning Web Development for fun this Summer (2014).

One of my favorite hobbies, cooking, started with me taking care of my younger brother. As the oldest brother of my home, and with my parents out working at night, it is my job to take care and cook dinner for my brother. At first I knew nothing about cooking, but I knew there existed the internet, a beautiful place where people from all over the world share their knowledge, so that's where I looked. It began with simple dishes such as making rice and eggs, but eventually lead me to exploring other options such as Indian, Japanese, Italian, and a whole bunch more types of food! The feeling of being able to make delicious food for those you care about is great! However, with all this knowledge on cooking, I felt a need to share it with someone, so I started teaching my brother how to cook as well. Now that I'm in college, I know that I don't have to worry since my brother is able to cook for himself.

My experiences are not limited to just cooking. For example, in middle school, I fell in love with Tennis, which lead to me spending hours and hours learning different Tennis techniques, learning training methods to make me better at the sport, and familiarizing myself with the rules as well. After I research, I would practice for hours. Once I learn something, the best way to familiarize myself with it is through actual experience such as practicing what I learned. It was frustrating at first whenever I learn new things, but soon I realized the only way I can go is up and through practice, I can only get better! Even video games, I would research first before I played. Eventually, I ended up learning a bunch of other things such as boxing, volleyball, and martial arts too. Although I may be focused on learning different coding languages as of now, I like to spend my free time exploring new hobbies such as magic tricks since being able to learn and try new things out is really fun! Being able to learn and share knowledge is extremely powerful, and not only is it powerful, it's what makes the world go evolve. Due to my love for learning, I was able to learn a huge variety of things, helping to expand my world.
